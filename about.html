DANKIRA WORLD CUP THE GREATEST SPORT EVENT IN CAR RACING 2022

In 10 years, Dankira Cup has rapidly established as Addis Ababa’s leading best lifestyle and sports event of the year. Nationally broadcast on TV and globally distributed, Dankira World Cup attracts A-list celebrity attendees, influencers and avid fans to catch a glimpse of the sport’s most iconic event of the year.

Launched in 2012 as part of the race revolution movement to excite, motivate and modernize the sport of racing for a new wave of fans, the Dankira Cup provides lucrative opportunities for racing car owners and trainers.
